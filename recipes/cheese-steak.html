Cheese Steak

Description

Philly Cheesesteaks are delicious. Eat them. Not Healthy. Heart Attack.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. Hoagie Bread
  • 5 tons Ribeye Steak
  • 0 Mayo
  • Your Favorite Cheese (has to melt)
  • Any Kind of Veggies. Preferably peppers and onion

Steps

  1. Make bread
  2. Cook steak
  3. Cheese on steak while the steak in pan
  4. Cook veggies
  5. Combine in hoagie
